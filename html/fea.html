Abaqus Finite Element Software

In my ME341 course, Intermediate Mechanics of Materials, I was introduced to finite element software. We used Abaqus, a popular finite element software, to create model 3D objects and how stresses flow through them when they are loaded. In a heat transfer course we developed simple FEA programs of our own to show heat transfer through an object.

The FEA project within my Mechanics of Materials course focused mainly on stress concentration factors in 2 dimensional loading. You can see the exam report I was asked to write here.
