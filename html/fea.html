Abaqus Finite Element Software

In my ME341 course, Intermediate Mechanics of Materials, I was introduced to finite element software. We used Abaqus, a popular finite element software, to create model 3D objects and how stresses flow through them when they are loaded.

The courses I have taken which covered applications of FEA have mostly covered fundementals of numerical theory, rather than focusing on a single program.
